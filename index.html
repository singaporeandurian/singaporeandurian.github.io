LEARN ABOUT US

BITRING is a cross chain experience merging ETH and BTC NFT technology. Our first 333 Ordinals are a low # (60- 99k) btc NFTs numbered and inscribed with generative art by the leader of the Underground. Items 334-1000 will be inscribed upon claim. ETH BITRINGS can be used to claim a BITRING Ordinal on the Ordinals protocol using the burn-to-claim mechanism below.

Similar to Unordinals, each piece of the series will be securely inscribed on the immutable Bitcoin blockchain, where the inscription order is determined by your token ID.

JOURNAL

Telegraph Fashion UK

Another gem shot by Vava Ribeiro in February 2020 (just before the world changed forever!) This editorial in Arpoador, Rio, was for the fashion supplement of British national newspaper Telegraph. Styled by Dan May and featuring four boys street-casted and now represented by Squad (it happens a lot): Bernardo Gomes, Matheus Robert, João Victor and Matheus Pereira. These boys are going far!

Luka & Aghata for Port Magazine

Rio is one of the best places in the world to create gorgeous, dreamy shoots, and one of the best places in the world for casting. We’re going to post a series of shoots in Rio with casting by Thais Mendes from Squad, and this one, shot end of 2019 for British print publication PORT Magazine is the first one. Staring beautiful couple Luka D’Saint and Agatha Lima, both represented by Squad Brazil, shot by Vava Ribeiro, styled by Dan May and produced by Two Palms Productions.

Replica Magazine – UK

It’s not just Rio that keeps on giving! São Paulo is also a mecca of youth culture, and we’re all over that. These candid images, shot by Vava Ribeiro in Praça Roosevelt, São Paulo, features boys street-casted by us in 2019 – and some of them are now represented by Squad Brazil. If you’re looking to shoot in Brazil (or anywhere else in Latin America), give us a shout! We’re the leading street casting agency in the south hemisphere, with an unmatched eye for edgy, diverse beauty.

Man About Town – UK

Rio keeps on giving ! These golden-skinned friends playing in overcast Arpoador, Rio, were street-casted by Thais Mendes for British fashion print magazine Man About Town, shot by John Balsom, styled by Dan May and produced by Two Palms Productions. Staring Bernardo Gomes, Matheus Pereira and Manuela Coelho, now represented by Squad Brazil.
